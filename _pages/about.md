My name is Dana Hagist. I currently work as a Data Scientist at LinkedIn, focusing on our marketing business.

Originally from Santa Cruz, CA, I'm passionate about the outdoors and just about anything that is either physically or mentally challenging. I train Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, compete in endurance events (marathons and triathlons), and spend most of the rest of my time reading or working on data analytics in Python and R.

I have an academic background (Master's Degree) in Economics, and am working tirelessly to elevate my Computer Science skills. I started this blog to share my journey into the Data Science world — collaborating, sharing insight, and learning from others.

Thank you for checking out the site. Please reach out if there is anything I can do to help you on your journey, or if you have any constructive advice for mine.
